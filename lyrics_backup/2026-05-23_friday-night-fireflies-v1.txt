[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Midnight driving with my hand resting high on your thigh
Just the rhythm of the road underneath the dark sky
You're looking at me with a slow-burning heat in your eyes
Lighting up the darkness like Friday night fireflies

[Verse 1]
We pulled off the highway and down to the creek
The water is rushing, but we hardly speak
I can feel the warm breeze tangling up in your hair
There's a beautiful chemistry filling the air

[Pre-Chorus]
Come a little closer, let the engine cool down
We're a million miles away from the noise of the town
Your skin is like honey under the pale yellow moon
I just want to taste you, lost in this midnight tune

[Chorus]
You're my Friday night fireflies glowing in the dark
Leaving a trail of magic straight into my heart
I’m pulling you close in the warmth of your sweet glow
Wrap your body around me and let the country wind blow

[Post-Chorus]
Yeah, glowing in the dark with you by my side
Riding this feeling on a late-night country ride

[Verse 2]
I pour us a drink from a bottle of rye
We're tracing the constellations up in the sky
Your body against me, my lips on your neck
This is a craving I never want to forget

[Pre-Chorus 2]

[Bridge]
Glowing in the dark, you're the light of my soul
Through the silence and shadows, you make me whole
I feel the fire burning when our bodies align
Every echo of truth says our destiny is intertwined
Say my name softly, keep Saeed in your mind
[Whispered] (Saeed… Saeed…)

[Spoken]
